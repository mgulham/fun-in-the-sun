Learn about your Skin Type

In the U.S., more than 9,500 people are diagnosed with skin cancer every day. About 90 percent of nonmelanoma skin cancers are associated with exposure to ultraviolet (UV) radiation from the sun. By increasing your knowledge of the UV index of your location, you can be prepared to limit your exposure.
